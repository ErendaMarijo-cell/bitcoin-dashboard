About Bitcoin Wallet Analysis

Bitcoin wallet analysis enables the aggregation of multiple on-chain addresses into a unified analytical view. Because Bitcoin wallets often distribute funds across many addresses, analyzing a single address rarely reflects the full capital structure of an entity.

By combining balances, unspent transaction outputs (UTXOs), transaction histories, and address interaction patterns, wallet analysis reveals capital concentration, spending behavior, and long-term holding dynamics.

Multi-address wallet exploration is widely used for institutional treasury tracking, exchange reserve monitoring, whale activity detection, and forensic blockchain analysis.

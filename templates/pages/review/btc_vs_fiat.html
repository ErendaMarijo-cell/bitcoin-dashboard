About Bitcoin Value vs Fiat Currencies

This chart tracks Bitcoin’s long-term price and purchasing power relative to major global fiat currencies including USD, EUR, JPY, GBP, CHF, AUD, CAD, NZD, CNY, INR, SGD, RUB, TRY, ARS, BRL, and ZAR.

By comparing Bitcoin against multiple monetary systems, this dashboard highlights how BTC performs as a long-term store of value relative to inflationary and non-inflationary currencies alike.

Because Bitcoin is globally priced in US Dollars, its valuation in other currencies reflects both BTC market cycles and foreign exchange movements.

Users can explore Bitcoin price performance across full history, individual years, halving cycles, or custom time ranges.

The logarithmic default scale reflects Bitcoin’s exponential growth over time.

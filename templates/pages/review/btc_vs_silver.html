Bitcoin vs Silver (troy ounce)

About Bitcoin vs Silver

This chart measures Bitcoin’s long-term valuation relative to physical silver, priced in troy ounces per BTC.

By denominating Bitcoin in silver rather than fiat currency, the chart provides insight into Bitcoin’s purchasing power relative to one of the world’s most widely traded monetary and industrial metals.

Silver has historically functioned as both currency metal and commodity, bridging monetary and industrial demand. Comparing Bitcoin to silver therefore highlights Bitcoin’s performance not only as a store of value, but also relative to broader commodity markets.

When Bitcoin rises in silver terms, BTC is gaining purchasing power relative to silver. When it falls, silver is outperforming Bitcoin.

Users can explore Bitcoin’s valuation in silver across full history, individual years, halving cycles, or custom date ranges.

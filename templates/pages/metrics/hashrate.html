About Bitcoin Hashrate

Bitcoin hashrate represents the total computational power actively securing the network. It reflects how much mining hardware is currently engaged in validating transactions and producing new blocks.

Rising hashrate indicates increasing miner participation, capital investment, and network security, while declining hashrate typically signals miner shutdowns, energy cost pressure, or short-term operational stress.

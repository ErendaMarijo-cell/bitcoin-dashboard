Bitcoin Value vs Fiat Currencies

About Bitcoin Value vs Fiat Currencies

This chart tracks Bitcoin’s long-term price, exchange rate, and purchasing power across major global fiat currencies.

Users can analyze Bitcoin price performance in currencies such as US Dollar (USD), Euro (EUR), Japanese Yen (JPY), British Pound (GBP), Swiss Franc (CHF), Chinese Yuan (CNY), Indian Rupee (INR), Turkish Lira (TRY), Argentine Peso (ARS), Brazilian Real (BRL), and many others.

By comparing Bitcoin against multiple monetary systems, this dashboard reveals how BTC performs as a store of value relative to both stable reserve currencies and inflation-prone fiat currencies.

For example, users can explore how the Bitcoin price in Turkish Lira, Bitcoin price in Argentine Peso, or Bitcoin price in Brazilian Real has evolved over time — highlighting the impact of currency debasement, inflation, and foreign exchange fluctuations.

Because Bitcoin is globally priced in US Dollars, its valuation in other currencies reflects both BTC market cycles and FX rate movements.

Time range filters allow analysis across full history, individual years, halving cycles, or fully custom date ranges.

The logarithmic default scale visualizes Bitcoin’s exponential monetization curve across global fiat systems.
